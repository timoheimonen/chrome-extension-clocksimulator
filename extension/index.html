How to use

Controls

ControlDescription
Theme (moon/sun icon)Switch between dark and light mode
Keep screen on (monitor icon)Prevent the display from sleeping
Tick seconds (stopwatch icon)Switch between ticking and smooth second hand
Info (ⓘ button)Show help and contact info

Screen burn-in protection

To protect OLED and AMOLED displays, the clock subtly shifts its position every 10 minutes in a slow circular pattern. Dark Mode further reduces the risk of burn-in.

Chrome Extension

This extension replaces your new tab page with a beautiful analog clock. All settings are saved locally in your browser.
